    Silver dental fillings, which are frequently used to restore teeth, can be substituted with cosmetic dental fillings. Composite resins, porcelain, and inomers are used to make tooth-colored cosmetic fillings. A person’s cosmetic fillings are invisible when they speak or smile.

    Various kinds of aesthetic fillings:

    Composite fillings

    Ceramic or porcelain fillings

    Fluoride particles are released from cosmetic fillings to stop further deterioration. Because cosmetic fillings resemble natural teeth in appearance, they mix in well with the teeth.
